Noise filter ⓘ
Noise Filter
Scores each post from 0 (clean) to 10+ (noisy) using 10 independent signals:
  • Content length — posts under 20 chars (+5)
  • Account age — accounts under 7 days old (+2–5)
  • Hashtag count — 3+ hashtags suggest tag-stuffing (+2–4)
  • Engagement — zero likes/reposts/replies (+1); real engagement (−1 bonus)
  • Solicitation — follow-farming phrases (+5)
  • Reply — replies in search results rarely have news value (+3)
  • Language — non-English posts (+3)
  • URL-only — post is just a link with no context (+4)
  • Mentions — more than 3 @mentions suggests spam (+2–4)
  • Repeat handle — same account posting 3+ times in one batch (+2–3)
The slider sets the hide threshold (default 3). Posts above the threshold are filtered out entirely. Posts within 2 points below it are dimmed. Priority news accounts are never filtered.
