Expiry & Price Strategies
Near Expiry
Markets closing within a few days with price between 25–75¢ — genuinely undecided outcomes with time pressure.
Extreme
Markets closing very soon priced near 0¢ or 100¢ — near-certainty outcomes. Useful for asymmetric bets if a contradicting event fires.
Signal
High semantic match score + undecided price (20–80¢). The news matches this market but the price hasn't moved yet — the opportunity window.
Tension
High match score + closing within 7 days + price 30–70¢. Highest urgency: the event is live, the market expires soon, and the price is still open.
